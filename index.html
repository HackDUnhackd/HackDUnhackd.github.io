About

We are proud to announce a unqiue hackathon, Hack D UnhackD' where anyone can participate. Hack D UnhackD' is organized by the students of IIIT-Naya Raipur. Aim of this event is to serve the development of both programming and hardware community by exploiting the potential of young and worthy developers. Right from a Newbie to a Veteran, all can take advantage of this platform to learn, build, and create stuff from scratch.

FAQ's

Registration - as a team or individually ?
You can do either of them. If you do not have a team member, you will be assigned as a team with other individual participants on the event commencement day itself.
Who can Participate?
Anyone having a valid student ID card of their college or university is eligible to take part in the competition.
Can a team have members from different colleges?
Yes.
How would I know whether I am selected or not?
We will revert back to you with e-mails provided on the registration form.
Will I be provided with accomodation?
Yes, participants selected for the event will be hosted in the IIIT-NR campus. Food and accomodation for the entire duration of the event will be provided for the team members only.
When will the registeration open?
The registration will commence from 7 February, 2018 and will be open till 10 March, 2018.
How many persons can be there in one team?
One team can have a minimum of 1 and maximum of 3 members.
Having any other query
The HDU team is eagerly waiting for you on Facebook, Twitter or mail us instead.
